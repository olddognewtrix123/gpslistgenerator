Enter your semicolon-delimited list of addresses and click the Get Coordinates button.

Remember, your semicolon-delimited list of addresses needs to formatted like this:

"7301 Buttercup Rd,Sykesville Maryland;7555 Waterloo Rd,Jessup Maryland;30420 Revells Neck Rd,Westover Maryland"

The list can be as long as you like. Please note that a GPS coordinate gets returned once every two seconds,

so you will not get your results all at once. The delay guarantees the accuracy of the results from Google's API.

Sorry, there's only the raw data on the screen and no nicely formatted data export.

You will have to copy your list of GPS coordinates straight from the page and handle formatting yourself.

The fact that the list is semicolon-delimited should enable you to do this easily in Excel or some similar application
